Q:We tried other solutions and they didn't understand construction.

Valid concern. Here's how this is different. Week 1 is onboarding. You tell us:

Valid concern. Here's how this works. You control access completely:

  1. —Role-based permissions — we only access folders you explicitly grant
  2. —You can revoke access instantly if needed
  3. —We work in your existing cloud storage (Google Drive, SharePoint), not downloading to separate systems

We provide:

  1. —Activity logging — every action is timestamped and recorded in your system
  2. —Signed NDAs with clear data handling procedures
  3. —No data retention post-contract — everything stays in your systems

We access your Google Drive or SharePoint with the same permission controls you'd give an internal employee. You have full visibility and control at all times.

Q:What if our subs won't respond to you?

We use a documented chase protocol with clear escalation at every step:

  1. 1Email request with a clear deadline sent to sub
  2. 2Follow-up email after 5 days if no response
  3. 3Phone call if still no response after 10 days
  4. 4Escalated to you after 3 documented attempts — you decide what happens next

The benefit: you discover non-compliance 30 days before expiry instead of during an inspection or audit.
